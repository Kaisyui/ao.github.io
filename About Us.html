Rendering in the context of computer graphics refers to the process of generating
a visual representation of a digital model or scene. AutoCAD is a computer-aided design (CAD) software developed by Autodesk.

and documentation.
Modeling, in computer graphics, refers to the process of creating digital representations
of objects or environments using specialized software. This can involve creating 3D models from scratch or using techniques such as scanning
and photogrammetry to capture real-world objects and convert them into digital form.
